Our Story

Defining the standard for luxury seating in India.

The Journey

A Legacy of Uncompromising Comfort

Since our inception, Recliners India has been driven by a singular vision: to transform the way India relaxes. We recognized a gap in the market for truly premium, ergonomically engineered seating that doesn't compromise on aesthetic appeal.

From our humble beginnings to becoming the premier luxury furniture brand, our journey has been defined by our relentless pursuit of perfection, utilizing world-class materials and pioneering designs.

Our Mission: To provide unparalleled relaxation experiences through innovative design and superior craftsmanship.

The Difference

Why Choose Recliners India

Premium Materials

We source only the finest top-grain leathers and high-density foams from around the world to ensure lasting luxury.

Ergonomic Engineering

Every curve and mechanism is rigorously tested and designed to provide optimal lumbar support and posture alignment.

Unmatched Warranty

We stand by our craftsmanship with an industry-leading 5-year warranty on frames and mechanisms.

Our Art

Mastering Craftsmanship

Design Philosophy

Our designs blend contemporary aesthetics with timeless comfort. Each piece undergoes a rigorous design process where our engineers and artists collaborate to create silhouettes that look stunning in any space while providing a sanctuary of relaxation.

Attention to Detail

From the precision of our automated cutting machines to the delicate hand-stitching by our master upholsterers, no detail is overlooked. The seamless integration of our motorized mechanisms ensures a whisper-quiet, smooth transition into your perfect resting position.
